DaScout Emailing
Runs entirely in your browser — no server, no account to create

Marketing emails, sent from your own Gmail

DaScout Emailing is a free composer for small campaigns. Design a branded email with a live preview — logos, gradient backgrounds, your colors and fonts — paste your recipient list, and every person gets their own individual copy, sent by your Gmail account rather than a third-party service.

Uses Google's official sign-in. Your password never touches this site, and you can revoke access anytime in your Google account settings.

How it works

1Sign in with Google

You grant one permission: sending email on your behalf. Nothing is read from your inbox, and the sign-in stays on this device.

2Compose with live preview

Header and footer logos, gradient or solid backgrounds, text colors and sizes — the preview is exactly what recipients receive.

3Test, then send

Send a test to yourself first. Then send the campaign — each recipient gets an individual copy, with live progress and a per-recipient report.

Why it's safe

Your Gmail does the sending

Mail goes out through Google's official API from your own account — replies land in your inbox, and your sending reputation stays yours.

Nothing leaves your browser

There is no server and no database. Drafts save locally on your device; recipient lists are never uploaded anywhere.

Recipients stay private

Every send is individual — nobody sees anyone else's address, unlike a CC/BCC blast.

Good to know: Gmail limits sending to roughly 500 recipients per day on personal accounts (about 2,000 on Google Workspace) — right-sized for small campaigns, not mass blasts. Images are embedded inside the email itself, so nothing depends on external hosting. If a limit is hit mid-send, the run stops cleanly and one click resumes the remainder later.
